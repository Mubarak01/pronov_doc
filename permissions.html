Permissions

User's role determines what they can do. Each role has a default permission sets.

Our default permissions are as follows:

  1. Anyone can register to create a store (with or without branches).
  2. Anyone who register the store is taken as the owners/managers are treated as the super-admin.
  3. Only super-admin are allowed to invite others and add store keepers.
  4. Owners/Managers have full rights to all contents on our app. Both the ones they creates and that of others within them.
  5. Store keepers have full rights to contents they own. They can only edit, not delete.
  6. Contributors have full right to add and edit contents they uploaded to our app- no delete.
  7. Consumers have view-only rights. They cannot create contents.
Our default permission matrix (in categories) is as follows...
General
  • Browse tree

    • Allows users to perform document Management actions. Our system automatically blocks users without Permission.

  • Read Content

    • Allows users access contents - read and preview.

  • Check in any document

    • Authorizes users of certain role to access some actions and activities. Also allows them to view and review properties.

  • Create

    • Allows users to create branches and groups.

  • Modify

    • Grants users the following:

    1. Add content(s)
    2. Edit content(s)
    3. Adjust and/or change settings preferences
    4. Adjust security level
  • Delete

    • Allows users to delete any document in the store.

  • Destroy

    • Allows users to delete content(s) and/or document(s) without an UNDO option.

  • Manage workflow

    • Allows and gives approval of users who can modify, check in document(s), create, delete and destroy anything.

  • Modify Permissions

    • Allows users the role to manage document-level permissions of any document, from their properties to security.
